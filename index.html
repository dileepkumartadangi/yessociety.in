About YESS

Youth Empowerment Service Society (YESS) is dedicated to empowering youth and women to build strong, self-reliant communities through education, leadership, and sustainable development initiatives.

Youth Empowerment Service Society (YESS) is a registered non-profit organization committed to advancing community development in Parvathipuram Manyam District, Andhra Pradesh. The organization works closely with tribal and rural communities to improve access to education, promote skill development, and create sustainable livelihood opportunities.

Through community-driven initiatives, YESS empowers youth and women to become leaders of positive change. By fostering leadership, social inclusion, and capacity building, the organization strives to create resilient communities and ensure long-term sustainable development.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive society where empowered youth and women lead sustainable social and economic development.

Our Mission

To uplift marginalized communities by promoting education, entrepreneurship, leadership development, and life skills.
